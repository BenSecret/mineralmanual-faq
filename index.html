About Mineral Manual

Mineral Manual is an educational mineralogy app with illustrated reference pages, quizzes, learning games, bookmarks, progress tracking, and a personal collection feature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Mineral Manual work offline?

Yes. Core reference content, images, quizzes, bookmarks, and progress are stored on your device.

Where is my quiz progress saved?

Quiz progress, points, streaks, unlocked levels, bookmarks, and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

What is My Collection?

My Collection lets you build your own mineral collection using entries, notes, and photos selected from your device. This content stays on your device and is not uploaded by Mineral Manual.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. Mineral Manual does not require an account and does not transmit personal information to external servers.

Can I suggest corrections or new content?

Yes. Please email any corrections, mineral suggestions, bug reports, or feedback using the contact email above.

I found a bug. What should I include?

Please include your device model, whether you are using iPhone or Android, the app version if known, and a short description of what happened.
