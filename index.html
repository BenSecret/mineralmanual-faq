Contact

About SaltShack Minerals

SaltShack Minerals is an educational mineralogy app with illustrated reference pages, quizzes, learning games, bookmarks, progress tracking, and a personal collection feature.

Frequently Asked Questions

I found an error or missing information. Can I report it?

Yes. Corrections are welcome, including mineral details, images, quiz questions, spelling mistakes, or factual errors.

The app is not working properly. What should I include?

Please include your device model, whether you are using iPhone or Android, the app version if known, and a short description of what happened.

Does SaltShack Minerals require an account?

No. SaltShack Minerals does not require an account or sign-in.

Does SaltShack Minerals collect personal data?

No. SaltShack Minerals does not collect or transmit personal user data to external servers.

Where is my progress and collection data stored?

Quiz progress, bookmarks, preferences, and My Collection entries are stored locally on your device.

Will My Collection photos be uploaded?

No. Photos and notes added to My Collection remain on your device and are not uploaded by SaltShack Minerals.

Can I request new features?

Yes. Feature suggestions and feedback are welcome by email.
